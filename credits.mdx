## How Credits Work

Each API call costs a fixed number of credits. New accounts start with **10 free credits**.

## Costs by Endpoint

| Endpoint | Method | Credits |
|----------|--------|---------|
| `/api/v1/research/search` | POST | 1 |
| `/api/v1/citations/resolve` | POST | 1 |
| `/api/v1/citations/search` | POST | 1 |
| `/api/v1/citations/{case_id}` | GET | 2 |
| `/api/v1/citations/{case_id}/cited-by` | GET | 1 |
| `/api/v1/citations/{case_id}/citations-of` | GET | 1 |

Credit cost is per call, regardless of result size.

## Checking Your Balance

View remaining credits in your [dashboard](https://app.vaquill.ai/settings). When credits reach zero, API calls return `403`.

## Plans

| Plan | Credits | Best For |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Free** | 10 | Evaluation |
| **Starter** | 100/month | Individual developers |
| **Professional** | 500/month | Small teams |
| **Business** | 2,000/month | Production use |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | High-volume integrations |

## Expiration

Monthly credits reset on your billing cycle date. Unused credits do not roll over.

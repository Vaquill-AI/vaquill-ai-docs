## How Credits Work

Each API call costs a fixed number of credits depending on the endpoint. New accounts receive **10 free API credits** to get started.

## Credit Costs by Endpoint

| Endpoint | Method | Credits |
|----------|--------|---------|
| `/api/v1/research/search` | POST | 1 |
| `/api/v1/citations/resolve` | POST | 1 |
| `/api/v1/citations/search` | POST | 1 |
| `/api/v1/citations/{case_id}` | GET | 2 |
| `/api/v1/citations/{case_id}/cited-by` | GET | 1 |
| `/api/v1/citations/{case_id}/citations-of` | GET | 1 |

<Note>
  Credit costs are fixed per call regardless of result size. A search returning 1 result costs the same as one returning 50.
</Note>

## Checking Your Balance

Monitor your remaining credits from your [dashboard](https://app.vaquill.ai/settings). When your balance reaches zero, API calls return a `403 Insufficient credits` error.

## Purchasing Credits

Additional credits can be purchased from your dashboard. Vaquill offers several subscription tiers:

| Plan | Included Credits | Best For |
|------|-----------------|----------|
| **Free** | 10 | Testing and evaluation |
| **Starter** | 100/month | Individual developers |
| **Professional** | 500/month | Small teams and applications |
| **Business** | 2,000/month | Production applications |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | High-volume integrations |

<Tip>
  If you're evaluating the API, the 10 free credits are enough to test every endpoint at least once.
</Tip>

## Credit Expiration

Monthly credits reset on your billing cycle date. Unused credits do not roll over to the next month.

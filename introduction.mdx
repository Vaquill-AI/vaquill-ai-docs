## What is Vaquill?

Vaquill AI is an intelligent legal research platform built for legal professionals working with Indian law. It combines advanced AI with a comprehensive legal corpus to help you find relevant case law, analyze documents, review contracts, and manage citations — in seconds instead of hours.

## The Legal Corpus

Vaquill indexes and maintains a comprehensive database of Indian case law:

- **Supreme Court** — 185,000+ cases
- **High Courts** — All 25 Indian High Courts covered
- **Citation Formats** — SCC, AIR, INSC
- **28M+ indexed chunks** for precise semantic search

## Key Capabilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Intelligent Document Search" icon="file-magnifying-glass">
    Upload contracts, case files, or court documents and ask questions in natural language. Vaquill understands legal context and returns precise answers with highlighted sources and exact page references.

    Supports 50+ file formats including PDF, DOCX, XLSX, and scanned documents with OCR.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Answer Verification" icon="shield-check">
    Every AI response goes through a 4-layer verification system:
    1. Exact match validation
    2. Citation verification
    3. Semantic matching
    4. LLM cross-check

    Each claim gets a confidence score — verified, partially verified, or unverified.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="15+ Legal Tools" icon="toolbox">
    A full suite of specialized tools including contract review, NDA triage, risk assessment, compliance checks against 11 regulatory frameworks (GDPR, DPDP Act, HIPAA, and more), document comparison, and redline analysis.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Citation Network" icon="diagram-project">
    Traverse the citation graph to see how cases cite each other. Track case freshness — identify overruled, distinguished, or questioned decisions. Supports 100+ citation styles.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Activation

Two ways to activate a tool:

1. **Tools menu:** Click the tools icon in the conversation bar
2. **Natural language:** Describe what you need

> *"Triage this NDA"* → NDA Triage
>
> *"Compare these two versions"* → Compare Documents
>
> *"Check this against GDPR"* → Compliance Check

## Overview

Vaquill includes a suite of specialized tools designed for common legal workflows. Each tool can be triggered from the tools menu or by describing what you need in natural language.

## Using Tools

You can activate a tool in two ways:

1. **Tools menu** — Click the tools icon in the conversation bar and select the tool you want
2. **Natural language** — Just describe what you need and Vaquill will select the appropriate tool:

> *"Triage this NDA"* → activates NDA Triage
>
> *"Compare these two versions"* → activates Compare Documents
>
> *"Check this against GDPR"* → activates Compliance Check

<Info>
  Tool availability depends on your subscription plan. Free accounts have access to a limited set of tools. See your [dashboard](https://app.vaquill.ai/settings) for details.
</Info>

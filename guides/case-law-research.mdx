## Overview

Vaquill gives you access to a comprehensive legal corpus with 1M+ cases across multiple courts and jurisdictions. Search using natural language, Boolean operators, or citation strings to find relevant precedents.

## Search Methods

### Natural Language Search

Describe what you're looking for in plain English:

> *"Cases where the court discussed the right to privacy as a fundamental right"*

Vaquill uses intelligent search to find cases that match the meaning of your query, not just keywords.

### Boolean Keyword Search

For precise control over search results, use Boolean operators:

| Operator | Example | Effect |
|----------|---------|--------|
| `AND` | `murder AND conviction` | Both terms must appear |
| `OR` | `Section 302 OR Section 304` | Either term can appear |
| `NOT` | `appeal NOT dismissed` | Excludes documents with the term |
| `"..."` | `"beyond reasonable doubt"` | Exact phrase match |
| `( )` | `(murder OR homicide) AND conviction` | Grouped conditions |

### Citation Search

Search directly by citation string:

> *"(2017) 10 SCC 1"* or *"AIR 2017 SC 4161"*

Vaquill resolves citation strings across formats — SCC, AIR, and INSC — and returns the matching case with full metadata.

## Filtering Results

Narrow your search using filters:

- **Court** — Filter by specific courts or tribunals
- **Year / Date Range** — Cases from a particular period
- **Judge** — Cases authored by or involving a specific judge
- **Citation Format** — Filter by SCC, AIR, or INSC citations

## Understanding Results

Each search result includes:

- **Case title and citation** — Full case name with all known citation strings
- **Court and date** — Which court decided it and when
- **Judges** — The bench composition
- **Relevance score** — How closely it matches your query
- **Snippet** — Key passage from the judgment matching your search

## Using the API

You can also run case law searches programmatically via the [Research Search](/api-reference) endpoint:

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.vaquill.ai/api/v1/research/search \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer vq_key_..." \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "query": "right to privacy fundamental rights",
    "court": "supreme_court",
    "year_from": 2015,
    "year_to": 2024,
    "limit": 10
  }'
```

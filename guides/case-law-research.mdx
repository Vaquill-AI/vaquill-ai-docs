## Search Methods

### Natural Language

Describe what you're looking for:

> *"Cases where the court discussed the right to privacy as a fundamental right"*

### Boolean Operators

| Operator | Example | Effect |
|----------|---------|--------|
| `AND` | `murder AND conviction` | Both terms must appear |
| `OR` | `Section 302 OR Section 304` | Either term can appear |
| `NOT` | `appeal NOT dismissed` | Excludes the term |
| `"..."` | `"beyond reasonable doubt"` | Exact phrase |
| `( )` | `(murder OR homicide) AND conviction` | Grouped conditions |

### Citation Lookup

Search by citation string directly:

> *"(2017) 10 SCC 1"* or *"AIR 2017 SC 4161"*

Citation strings are resolved across formats (SCC, AIR, INSC).

## Filters

- **Court:** Specific courts or tribunals
- **Year / Date Range:** Cases from a particular period
- **Judge:** Cases by a specific judge
- **Citation Format:** SCC, AIR, or INSC

## Result Fields

- **Case title and citation:** All known citation strings
- **Court and date**
- **Judges:** Bench composition
- **Relevance score**
- **Snippet:** Matching passage from the judgment

## API

The case law search API lets you embed legal research directly into your product. Common use cases:

- **Contract drafting platforms** that surface relevant precedents during drafting
- **Practice management tools** with built-in case law lookup
- **Compliance systems** that cross-reference regulations against case law
- **Internal legal portals** for enterprise legal teams

### Search Endpoint

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.vaquill.ai/api/v1/research/search \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer vq_key_..." \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "query": "right to privacy fundamental rights",
    "court": "supreme_court",
    "year_from": 2015,
    "year_to": 2024,
    "limit": 10
  }'
```

## Overview

Document Search lets you upload legal documents — contracts, case files, court orders, agreements — and ask questions in natural language. Vaquill reads the document, understands legal context, and returns precise answers with highlighted sources and page references.

## Supported File Formats

Vaquill processes 50+ file formats:

| Category | Formats |
|----------|---------|
| Documents | PDF, DOCX, DOC, TXT, RTF, ODT |
| Spreadsheets | XLSX, XLS, CSV |
| Presentations | PPTX, PPT |
| Images | PNG, JPG, TIFF (processed with OCR) |
| Email | EML, MSG |
| Archives | ZIP (extracts and processes contents) |

<Tip>
  Scanned PDFs and images are automatically processed with OCR. Vaquill cleans OCR artifacts to reduce noise and improve answer quality.
</Tip>

## How to Use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a conversation">
    Open a new conversation from your [dashboard](https://app.vaquill.ai). You can also work within an existing Matter workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload documents">
    Drag and drop one or more files into the conversation. You'll see a progress indicator as each file is processed and indexed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask questions">
    Type your question in natural language. Examples:

    - *"What is the termination clause in this agreement?"*
    - *"Does this contract have a non-compete provision?"*
    - *"Summarize the key obligations of the licensee"*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review verified answers">
    Each response includes source citations pointing to the exact section of your document. Verification badges indicate the confidence level of each claim.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Retrieval Strategies

Vaquill uses multiple retrieval strategies to find the most relevant information:

- **Hybrid Search** — Combines keyword and semantic matching
- **HyDE** — Hypothetical Document Embeddings for better semantic recall
- **Multi-Query** — Expands your question into multiple search queries
- **Multi-Hop** — Chains reasoning across multiple document sections

The system automatically selects the best strategy for your query.

## Conversational Follow-ups

You can ask follow-up questions without re-uploading or re-stating context. Vaquill maintains conversation history and understands references to previous answers:

> **You:** What are the payment terms in this contract?
>
> **Vaquill:** The contract specifies net-30 payment terms with a 2% late fee...
>
> **You:** Are there any exceptions to that?
>
> **Vaquill:** Yes, Section 4.3 provides an exception for force majeure events...

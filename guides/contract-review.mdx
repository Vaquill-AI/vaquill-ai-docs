## Overview

Vaquill's contract review tools analyze agreements clause by clause, identify risks and missing provisions, check compliance against regulatory frameworks, and generate redline suggestions — all in seconds.

## Contract Review Tool

Upload a contract and Vaquill will produce a comprehensive analysis:

- **Clause-by-clause breakdown** with severity ratings
- **Risk identification** — unfavorable terms, ambiguous language, missing protections
- **Redline suggestions** — proposed changes with explanations
- **Summary** — executive overview of key terms and obligations

### How to Use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your contract">
    Drag and drop a contract (PDF, DOCX, or other supported formats) into a conversation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Contract Review tool">
    Choose **Contract Review** from the tools menu, or simply ask: *"Review this contract"*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the analysis">
    Vaquill returns a structured breakdown with each clause analyzed for risk, missing elements, and suggested modifications.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export results">
    Export the review to Word (DOCX) with tracked changes, or download as PDF or Markdown.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## NDA Triage

For non-disclosure agreements, the **NDA Triage** tool provides rapid screening with a pass/warn/fail rating system.

<Frame caption="NDA Triage with 10-criteria screening, pass/warn/fail ratings, and routing recommendations">
  <img src="/images/nda-triage.jpeg" alt="NDA Triage" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

It checks for:

- Scope of confidential information
- Duration and survival clauses
- Permitted disclosures and carve-outs
- Non-solicitation provisions
- Remedies and indemnification
- Governing law and jurisdiction

## Risk Assessment

The **Risk Assessment** tool uses a 5x5 severity-likelihood matrix to categorize identified risks:

<Frame caption="Risk assessment with 5x5 severity-likelihood matrix">
  <img src="/images/risk-assessment.jpeg" alt="Risk Assessment" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

| | Rare | Unlikely | Possible | Likely | Almost Certain |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Critical** | Medium | High | High | Extreme | Extreme |
| **Major** | Low | Medium | High | High | Extreme |
| **Moderate** | Low | Medium | Medium | High | High |
| **Minor** | Low | Low | Medium | Medium | High |
| **Negligible** | Low | Low | Low | Medium | Medium |

Each risk is mapped to a specific clause in the document with a recommended action.

Skills are expert legal playbooks that give the AI structured methodologies for specific tasks — contract review, NDA screening, risk assessment, compliance checks, and more. Instead of freeform answers, the AI follows a proven framework with checklists, severity ratings, and domain-specific guidance.

## How to Use Skills

There are two ways to activate a skill:

### Slash Command (Recommended)

Type `/` in the chat input to open the skill menu. Select a skill, then type your question. The skill's framework guides the AI's response.

For example: Type `/`, select **Contract Review**, then paste your contract text or ask about a specific clause.

### Automatic Activation

In [Agent Mode](/guides/agent-mode), the AI can autonomously activate a skill when your question clearly matches one. For example, if you ask "Review this NDA for risks," the agent may activate the NDA Triage skill on its own.

## Example Usage

### Contract Review via Slash Command

1. Type `/` and select **Contract Review**
2. Upload or paste your contract
3. Ask: "Review this services agreement and flag high-risk clauses"
4. The AI produces a clause-by-clause analysis with severity ratings and suggested redlines

### Risk Assessment in Agent Mode

With [Agent Mode](/guides/agent-mode) enabled, simply ask:

> *"Assess the legal risks of this proposed joint venture with a Chinese company"*

The AI recognizes this as a risk assessment task, activates the skill, and produces a structured 5x5 risk matrix with regulatory considerations.

### Case Brief from a Judgment

1. Type `/` and select **Case Brief**
2. Ask: "Brief the judgment in Kesavananda Bharati v. State of Kerala"
3. The AI produces an IRAC-format brief with proper citations and ratio decidendi
